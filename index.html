Rush-Hour Relay — Support

Thanks for playing Rush-Hour Relay, a one-tap arcade road-crossing game. Read the signals, hop the gaps, and ride the green wave as far as you can.

Contact

For help, questions, or bug reports, email us at info@cybertrip.jp. We usually reply within a few business days.

Purchases

All purchases (Remove Ads, themes, and the bundle) are one-time, non-consumable products — there is no subscription. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, use Settings → Restore purchases inside the game to restore them.
